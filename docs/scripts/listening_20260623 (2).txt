# ── 人の態度を表す表現 ──

His attitude toward me is really harsh.
I think he might hate me.
His attitude toward me is really harsh. I think he might hate me.

He's been pretty cold towards me lately.
I think he might hate me.
He's been pretty cold towards me lately — I think he might hate me.

ここがポイントです。
前者は今まさに感じている態度のきつさ、後者は「最近になって変わった」という時間的変化のニュアンスがあります。

He's been pretty cold towards me lately — I think he might hate me.


# ── severe vs harsh ──

His attitude is really harsh.
His attitude is pretty cold.
His attitude is quite rough.

ここがポイントです。
severe は天候・病状・罰則など「深刻な厳しさ」に使います。人の態度・口調のきつさには harsh、cold、rough を使いましょう。

His attitude is really harsh.


# ── may vs might（主観的推測） ──

It may rain tomorrow.
He might hate me.

ここがポイントです。
may は客観的・公式的な可能性の表現、might は「もしかして〜かも」という個人の主観的な推測・不安に使います。口語では might の使用頻度が圧倒的に高いです。

He might hate me.


# ── make the mistake of -ing ──

I made the mistake of trusting him too much.
I made the mistake of telling her the truth.
I made the mistake of arriving too early.

I shouldn't have trusted him so much.
I shouldn't have told her that.
I shouldn't have gone there alone.

ここがポイントです。
make the mistake of -ing は「〜するというミスを犯す」という構文。口語の後悔表現では shouldn't have + 過去分詞 の方が頻度が高く、より自然に聞こえます。

I made the mistake of trusting him too much.
I shouldn't have trusted him so much.


# ── The same goes for / That applies to ──

The same goes for trusting his promises.
The same goes for his sister.
The same goes for everything else he said.

That applies to trusting his promises too.
That applies to everyone in the team.
That applies to all of us.

ここがポイントです。
The same goes for は口語でカジュアルに「同じことが〜にも当てはまる」と言いたい時に使います。That applies to はやや硬めで、説明的な会話やビジネスシーンにも使いやすい表現です。

The same goes for trusting his promises.


# ── 英語の主語省略について ──

I made the mistake of trusting him.
I should have known better.
I can't believe I trusted him.

# 英語では命令文を除き、主語を省略することはできません。
# 日本語の感覚で主語を落とすと文として成立しないので注意が必要です。


# ── このセッションの主要表現まとめ ──

His attitude toward me is really harsh. I think he might hate me.
He's been pretty cold towards me lately — I think he might hate me.
I made the mistake of trusting him too much.
I shouldn't have trusted him so much.
The same goes for trusting his promises.
That applies to trusting his promises too.
