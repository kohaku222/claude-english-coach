We're having a welcome party for the new B4 students today!
Haruna and I set everything up.
We ordered a ton of sushi because, hey, organizer's privilege — gotta make it count.
Can't wait!

# [Professional]
Today we're hosting a welcome reception for the incoming B4 students.
Haruna and I handled the planning, and we took full advantage of our organizer roles to order plenty of sushi.
Looking forward to it.

# [Super Native]
So we've got the B4 welcome thing today — Haruna and I ran the whole show.
Ordered way too much sushi on purpose.
Organizer perks, you know?
Stoked.

# ────────────────────────────────────────
# TOPIC 2: B4歓迎会の準備（SPEAK MODE）
# ────────────────────────────────────────

# [修正版]
Today we're having a welcome reception for the B4 students.
Haruna and I took care of all the prep.

# ここがポイントです。
# 「確定済みの近未来の予定」には will より現在進行形 be + V-ing が自然です。また、自分を含む場合は I を最後に置くのがマナーです。

Today we're having a welcome reception for the B4 students.

# [ネイティブ代替]
Haruna and I put the whole thing together for the B4 welcome party today.

# ここがポイントです。
# put ~ together は「一から企画・準備した」という労力と主体性を自然に含む表現です。took care of the prep より能動的な印象を与えます。

Haruna and I put the whole thing together for the B4 welcome party today.

# ────────────────────────────────────────
# TOPIC 3: ワールドカップ寝落ち（SPEAK MODE）
# ────────────────────────────────────────

# [修正版]
Today, at 5 AM, the Japan vs. Netherlands World Cup match was held.
I'd been really looking forward to it and tried to stay up, but by 4 AM I just couldn't keep it up and fell asleep.
I ended up missing the whole thing.

# [ネイティブ代替]
I was hyped for the Japan-Netherlands match at 5 AM, but I crashed around 4 and missed the whole game.

# ここがポイントです。
# hyped は「興奮して楽しみにしていた」、crashed は「そのまま寝落ちした」を表す口語表現です。2文を1文に圧縮してテンポよく状況が伝わります。

I was hyped for the Japan-Netherlands match at 5 AM, but I crashed around 4 and missed the whole game.

# ────────────────────────────────────────
# TOPIC 4: stay up vs stayed late — 使い分け
# ────────────────────────────────────────

I tried to stay up for the match.
I stayed late at the office to finish the report.

# ここがポイントです。
# stay up は「夜更かしして起きていようとする」、stayed late は「夜遅くまで（場所に）いた」という意味です。起きていようとする意図には stay up を使います。

I tried to stay up for the match.

# ────────────────────────────────────────
# TOPIC 5: keep it up vs keep up — 使い分け
# ────────────────────────────────────────

I couldn't keep it up.
I couldn't keep up with the pace.

# ここがポイントです。
# keep it up はある状態を維持し続けること、keep up with は何かについていくこと、という別の意味になります。

I couldn't keep it up.

# ────────────────────────────────────────
# TOPIC 6: ended up missing — 結果表現
# ────────────────────────────────────────

I ended up missing the whole thing.
I ended up falling asleep on the couch.
I ended up staying home instead.

# ────────────────────────────────────────
# TOPIC 7: 単語・フレーズ例文
# ────────────────────────────────────────

# reception（歓迎会・受付・電波）
The signal reception in the server room is terrible — I had to step outside just to take the call.
We're hosting a welcome reception for the new members on Friday.
The hotel reception opens at seven in the morning.

# way too much（度を超えて多い）
I ordered way too much food at the restaurant.
She spent way too much time on that one slide.
We've got way too many tabs open right now.

# protagonist（主人公・中心人物）
In most RPGs, you build the protagonist's stats from scratch.
She's the protagonist of her own story, not a supporting character.
The protagonist's motivation drives the entire plot forward.
