# glossary / grocery の発音と使い方

# glossary（用語集）

There's a glossary at the back of the book if you don't know what a word means.
Check the glossary if you come across any unfamiliar terms.
The textbook has a detailed glossary at the end of each chapter.

# grocery（食料雑貨・食料品店）

I need to stop by the grocery store on my way home.
She picked up a few things at the grocery store.
We're running low on groceries — I'll go shopping this afternoon.
